Synopsis

The purpose of this project was to develop and deliver demand-led and decentralised services of the Kenya Meteorological Department (KMD) in the counties of Kakamega, Siaya, Kisumu and Trans Nzoia.

Goals

To make a step change in the quality, accessibility and use of weather and climate information services at all levels of decision making for sustainable development in Africa.

Objectives

Increased and better use of weather and climate information to inform decision making and minimise risks at county to household level. This will reduce vulnerability and contribute to economic growth and a decline in poverty.
